Chat Peck Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We collect a minimal set of information to provide and enhance our services:

  • Email Addresses: Collected during user registration to create and manage user accounts.
  • Chat Messages: Messages exchanged between users to enable seamless communication within the Chat Peck application.

How We Use Your Information

We are committed to using your information responsibly and transparently:

  • Communication: Your email address is used to facilitate user-to-user communication within the application.
  • Enhancement: We analyze aggregated data to continuously improve and optimize the features of Chat Peck.

Data Security

Your privacy and the security of your data are our top priorities:

  • Secure Storage: Your data is stored securely, employing encryption and access controls to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Access Control: Strict access controls are in place to limit data exposure to authorized personnel only.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services to enhance our offerings, with each service governed by its own privacy policies:

  • Analytics: Anonymous data may be shared with analytics services to help us understand user behavior and improve our application.

Policy Changes

This policy may be updated to reflect changes in our practices. Please check this page periodically for the latest information.
